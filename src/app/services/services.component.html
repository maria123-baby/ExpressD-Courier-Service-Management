Our Services

Warehousing

ExpressD’s network of fulfillment centers (warehouses) are powered by our proprietary software and optimally integrated with our shipping network giving you the flexibility to optimize your storage cost versus speed to delivery.

Fast Delivery

ExpressD’s infrastructure and technology power our Express parcel service that caters to eCommerce marketplaces, D2C brands, omnichannel retailers, small and medium industries, large enterprises, and consumers.

Door to Door Delivery

ExpressD specializes in providing door-to-door delivery solutions. Our door-to-door services ensure that packages are picked up from the sender's location and delivered directly to the recipient's doorstep, offering convenience and peace of mind to our customers.

Tracking

ExpressD is a courier service company that offers tracking of shipments to its customers. With ExpressD's tracking service, customers can easily monitor the progress of their shipments from pickup to delivery.
